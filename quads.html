Squats

lmao you dont know how to do squats

  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart. Maintain back integrety and raise your chest
  • If using barbell, Grip the bar across shoulders and support on your upper back.
  • Bend your knees until your hips are below your knees (Quads are parallel to floor)
  • Raise body/bar up to original position, and exhale at the top
